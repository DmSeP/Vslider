Natural sugar

Natural sugars are found in whole, unprocessed foods - fruits, vegetables, nuts, legumes, milk. After eating an apple, in addition to glucose and fructose, you will get water, fiber, vitamins and minerals, as well as very few calories. Fiber slows down the absorption of sugar, so the blood glucose level will not soar after a pear, but after a chocolate bar, which sharply increases the blood sugar level, and then also drops sharply, causes fatigue and the desire to have a snack again. Such sharp fluctuations in blood sugar lead to negative consequences.

The benefits of fruit

The fruits of each fruit tree contain a lot of iron, potassium, magnesium, fiber, vitamins: A, B and C, PP and creatine.

They are a valuable source of vitamins, fiber and minerals, such as antioxidants - beta-carotene (vitamin A) and vitamin C, which protect cells from aging and disease.

The benefits of berries

Berries contain vitamins, antioxidants and fiber. Essential oils in some berries have medicinal properties, for example, antiseptic, diuretic, expectorant. Such berries are used as part of the complex treatment of diseases. For colds and acute respiratory infections, black currants, strawberries are brewed, raspberry jam is diluted in warm water.
